Edge Case Handlers

Objections
Someone already called about this
"Yeah, there's a lot of that. What did the other one get wrong?"
"How did you get my number?"
"Your shop came up when I was researching HVAC companies in [CITY]. Just Googled around."
"Is this a sales call?" / "Are you selling something?"
"Not really -- I'm a developer, not a salesman. I built something and I'm trying to find out if it's actually useful. Can I ask you one question?"
Got burned by AI / don't trust AI
"That's why I'm calling owners instead of guessing. What went wrong?"
Need to think about it
"Totally fair. Is it more the price, or whether the AI actually works?"

If it's whether it works: "95% answer rate in 30 days or full refund. You keep everything we built."
Questions
What does it do? (quick explainer)
"It answers calls when you can't, qualifies the lead, and texts you the details so you can call back and close the job. No more lost calls."
How is this different from an answering service?
"An answering service takes a name and number. This actually qualifies the call -- finds out what's wrong, how urgent it is, and texts you the full picture. You call back ready to close, not playing phone tag."
Do you have any customers?
"Not yet -- I'm looking for my first few shops to prove it works. That's why there's no setup fee and you get founding pricing. What's your biggest headache with phones?"
Pricing Edge Cases
Too expensive
"What are you paying for your answering service? This does 10x more. And there's a Lite version -- $600/mo, after-hours only."
What's the difference between the two tiers?
"Pro: bilingual, ServiceTitan booking, 24/7 or after-hours."

"Lite: after-hours and weekends only. Same AI, same lead texts -- no bilingual, no ServiceTitan, no daytime."
What if it doesn't work?
"95% answer rate in 30 days or full refund. You keep everything we built -- the AI, the phone number, the setup. Zero risk on your end."
That's a big commitment
"Tell you what -- one missed call at $3,500 pays for two months. If it doesn't catch at least two in the first 30 days, I'll refund you in full. You keep everything."
Free Pilot
What's the catch?
"No catch. I have a demo but no proof it works on real HVAC calls at volume. That proof is worth more than money right now. You run it 30 days, give me honest feedback. If it worked, I ask for a testimonial. No obligation to pay."
Why is it free?
"I need 2-3 real businesses running it before I feel right charging anyone. Once they're happy, I start charging. You'd be getting in before that."
I don't have time for setup
"Setup is 10 minutes on your end -- total. You answer a few questions, I build it, you don't touch it again."
