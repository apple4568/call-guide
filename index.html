Send me info — insistent → Done
"Totally — what's your email?"
Objections
I answer all my calls
"That's dedication. What happens at 10 PM on a Saturday?"
Everything's fine / don't miss many
"That's better than most. What's your setup?"
Then: "What about when you're on a job or it's late?"
Someone already called about this
"Yeah, there's a lot of that. What did the other one get wrong?"
Got burned by AI / don't trust AI
"That's why I'm calling owners instead of guessing. What went wrong?"
Need to think about it
"Totally fair. Is it more the price, or whether the AI actually works? Because if it's whether it works — 95% answer rate in 30 days or full refund. You keep everything we built."
Gatekeeper — not the owner
"Oh, you deal with the phones? You probably know more than the owner. What happens to calls after you close?"
Pricing

"Lite: after-hours and weekends only. Same AI, same lead texts — no bilingual, no ServiceTitan, no daytime."
